Sora 2 Pro is the higher-fidelity Sora option for more polished video output.

It is better suited for production-quality assets where quality matters more than turnaround time.

## Best For

- Marketing videos
- Ads and product visuals
- Higher-quality motion assets
- Production-oriented video generation

## Features

- Higher-fidelity output
- More stable generation
- Better for polished visuals
- Supports cinematic-style video work

## Choose This If

Choose Sora 2 Pro when you care more about quality than speed. If you need faster iteration or draft-quality concepts, [Sora 2](/integrations/sora-2) is usually the better choice.
