Sora 2 is optimized for speed and iteration.

It is a strong choice when you need draft-quality video quickly for prototyping, social content, or creative experimentation.

## Features

- Fast text-to-video generation
- Good for rapid iteration
- Supports first-frame reference images
- Useful for creative concepting

## Use Cases

- Social content generation
- Concept videos
- Creative prototyping
- Fast visual storytelling
