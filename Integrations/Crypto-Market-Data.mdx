Crypto Market Data powered by Alpha Vantage provides real-time and historical cryptocurrency market information.

## Features

- Real-time crypto prices
- Historical data
- Exchange rates
- Market cap information
- Technical indicators
- Multiple cryptocurrencies

## Use Cases

- Crypto tracking apps
- Portfolio tracking
- Trading platforms
- Market analysis tools
- Cryptocurrency dashboards
