title: 'ElevenLabs Sound Effects'
description: 'Generate sound effects from text prompts'

ElevenLabs Sound Effects allows you to generate sound effects from a text prompt.

This is great for videos and this is great for when you want to create a sound effect when you press a button in your app.

## Features

- Text-to-sound generation
- Diverse sound library
- Fast generation times
- Easy customization
- Perfect for interactive elements

## Use Cases

- Button click sounds
- Interactive app feedback
- Video production
- Game sound effects
- App notifications
