ElevenLabs Music is a great text-to-music model. It can generate songs with or without lyrics, whether it's for instrumentals, background music, a music video, or a funny meme.

You can generate music for any purpose you need.

## Features

- Text-to-music generation
- Lyrics support
- Multiple music styles
- Instrumental options
- Creative flexibility

## Use Cases

- Background music generation
- Instrumental creation
- Song composition
- Video soundtracks
- Creative content
