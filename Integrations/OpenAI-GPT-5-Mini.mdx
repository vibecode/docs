OpenAI GPT-5 Mini is a fast yet advanced AI chatbot developed by OpenAI. It's very good at a wide range of skills like chatting, content generation, coding, and normal conversational interactions.

This version of GPT-5 optimizes for speed.

## Features

- Fast response times
- Advanced language understanding
- Excellent for content generation
- Strong coding capabilities
- Great for conversational AI

## Use Cases

- Chatbot development
- Content generation
- Code assistance
- Customer support
- General conversation
