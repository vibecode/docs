---
title: "Deploy to App Store"
description: "Publish your Vibecode app to iOS App Store and Google Play Store"
---

When you are ready to publish your app on the App Store, simply do the following:

1. Press share at the top right
2. Select Get Started
3. If you have an apple developer account and have signed all documents required by apple, select the three checkboxes and press next
   1. If you have not signed up for an Apple Developer Program, select that button and follow the instructions to enroll
   2. If you don't have an Expo account, go to https://expo.dev/signup and sign up for an account
