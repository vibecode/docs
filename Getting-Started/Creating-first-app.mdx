## From first prompt to full-stack app

Vibecode allows you to take your ideas and turn them into an app...

And even get them on the App Store.

## To get started 

1. Download the app -> https://www.vibecodeapp.com
2. Press the plus sign
3. Type your app idea and wait for generation
4. Test your app
5. Edit your app by explaining what you want changed
