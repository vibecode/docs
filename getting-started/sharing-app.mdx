With Vibecode, you can share your app with anyone — no App Store submission required. Build your app, tap share, and send it to friends, family, or co-workers instantly.

## Sharing via App Clips

App Clips let anyone try your app immediately without installing anything. When someone opens your share link on an iPhone, a lightweight version of your app launches instantly.

1. Open your project in the Vibecode app
2. Tap the **Share** button
3. Send the link via Messages, WhatsApp, email, or any other app
4. Recipients tap the link and your app opens immediately

<Tip>App Clips are the fastest way to share. No downloads, no App Store — just a link.</Tip>

## Sharing via the Web

If your app is deployed to the web (see [Deploying Web Apps](/deploying/guide)), you can share the URL directly. Anyone with the link can use your app in their browser.

## Other Sharing Options

- **QR Code** — Generate a QR code for your app link so people can scan to try it
- **Social Media** — Share the link on X, Instagram, TikTok, or any platform
- **Embed** — If deployed as a web app, embed it on your website
