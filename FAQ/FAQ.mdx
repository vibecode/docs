# Frequently Asked Questions

## How long does it take to build an app with Vibecode?

It depends on the complexity of your app. Simple apps can be built in hours, while more complex apps with multiple features might take longer. The beauty of Vibecode is that you can iterate and refine quickly using natural language.

## Do I need coding experience to use Vibecode?

No! Vibecode is designed for everyone. You describe what you want, and the AI generates the code. However, if you do have coding experience, you can dive deeper and customize the generated code.

## Can I export my code after building with Vibecode?

Yes, you can download your complete source code and use it however you like. This gives you full control and flexibility over your app.
