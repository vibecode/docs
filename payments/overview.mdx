Monetization should be easy to find because it is a core part of shipping a real app.

In Vibecode, monetization spans four main areas:

- **Plans & credits** for understanding Vibecode billing and usage
- **Web payments** with Stripe
- **Mobile in-app purchases** with RevenueCat
- **Upgrade flows and paywalls** inside your app
