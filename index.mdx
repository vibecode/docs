**Your ideas deserve to be apps.** Vibecode lets you build fully functional apps using natural language — no coding required. Describe what you want, and our AI agent builds it for you in real-time.

Available as a **mobile app on iOS** and as a **web app** at [vibecodeapp.com](https://www.vibecodeapp.com), Vibecode is built for everyone — from first-time builders to experienced developers looking to move faster.

## What You Can Build

With Vibecode, your creativity is the only limit:

- **Productivity Apps** — To-do lists, note-taking apps, habit trackers
- **Social Apps** — Chat apps, community builders, messaging platforms
- **AI-Powered Apps** — Chatbots, image generators, voice assistants using built-in AI integrations
- **Content Apps** — Image generators, video editors, content creators
- **Utility Apps** — Weather apps, calculators, converters, timers
- **Entertainment** — Games, trivia apps, interactive experiences
- **Business Apps** — Custom workflows, data dashboards, client portals

## Why Vibecode?

- **No Coding Required** — Build apps using natural language prompts
- **Mobile + Web** — Build on your iPhone or in the browser at vibecodeapp.com
- **Powered by Claude Code** — Uses the most advanced AI coding agent available
- **Built-in Backend** — Add databases, authentication, and cloud storage with Vibecode Cloud
- **AI & API Integrations** — GPT, Gemini, image generation, maps, weather, and more — all billed through your credits at no markup
- **Share Instantly** — Share your app via a link before you ever touch the App Store
- **Deploy to App Store** — When you're ready, submit to the Apple App Store
- **Transparent Pricing** — $1 in credits = $1 in AI usage. No markup on AI costs.

## Customize With Pinch to Build

Once your app is generated, use the [Pinch to Build](/getting-started/pinch-to-build) menu (a long-press gesture on your phone) to customize and enhance your app:

- **Design Customization** — Adjust colors and visual styling
- **Add Haptics** — Include tactile feedback
- **Advanced Features** — Add sorting, categories, reminders, and more
- **Export & Deploy** — Connect via SSH to code editors like Cursor or download your source code
